How E-commerce Catalyst Works

Launch your online store in four simple steps

1

Describe Your Store

Tell us about your business, products, and design preferences using simple text prompts.

2

Generate with DeepSite v2

Our AI creates a stunning, responsive storefront in seconds based on your description.

3

Add Products & Integrate

Upload products and connect your preferred e-commerce solution with our guided setup.

4

Launch & Grow

Go live instantly and use our analytics to optimize your store for growth.

 # Describe your ideal store store_description = { "business_type": "artisanal coffee roaster", "style": "minimalist, earthy tones", "products": [ "single-origin beans", "coffee subscriptions", "brewing equipment" ], "features": [ "monthly subscription option", "brewing guides blog", "gift cards" ] } # Generate store with DeepSite v2 storefront = deepsite_v2.generate(store_description) # Connect e-commerce solution ecommerce.connect("snipcart", API_KEY) # Launch store storefront.launch()
